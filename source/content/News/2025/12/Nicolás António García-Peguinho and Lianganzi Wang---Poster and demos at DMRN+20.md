On Tuesday 16th December 2025, Nicolás António García-Peguinho and Lianganzi Wang presented their initial work related to their PhDs at the [Digital Music Research Network One-day Workshop 2025](https://www.qmul.ac.uk/dmrn/dmrn20/):

* “Validating Few-shot Bird Vocalisation Detection through Causal Explanations”, Nico García-Peguinho (Queen Mary University of London, UK), David Kelly, Melane Navaratnarajah, Hana Chockler (King’s College London, UK) and Anna Xambó Sedó (Queen Mary University of London, UK).
* “Towards Real-Time, Stable Mapping from Multimodal Sensing to Interpretable Timbre Axes”, Lianganzi Wang, Iran R. Roman and Anna Xambó Sedó (Queen Mary University of London, UK).

You can find out more on the [DMRN+20 Proceedings](https://www.qmul.ac.uk/dmrn/media/dmrn/DMRN-20-Abstracts-15-Dec.pdf).
